Demystifying Lending
In just 5 minutes customers received notification for loan credit in the bank accounts after clicking a button. The experience felt like magic to most, while others were amazed it could be possible and simple.

Sterling Bank undertook an audacious step to demystify the jinx of Lending in Nigeria.

I was part of the project to deliver a simple, fast and desirable experience to the customer.

Problem Statement
Lending in Nigeria is characterized as a tedious process for customers and risky for lenders. Customers complaint revolves around the experience during loan applications due to the tacky structure within organizations.

Over the years, the challenge has loomed, organizations record profit decline annually and the customer recounts a poor experience.
The solution
Specta provided the opportunity of lending to customers a 5 minutes experience eliminating human factor delays and automating the process end to end without stress and hassles of documentation.
Roles & Responsibility
I led the initial design of Spectra for versions 1 & 2 from conceptualization to implementation from October 2017 - March 2019.
‍
I collaborated with teams from Business and Technology. The comprised of six members from the Retail, Loan Monitoring, Strategy and Technology.
‍
In addition, I also worked as a Researcher, Content Strategist, UI Developer, and provided technical support to the Software Developer during the development of the product.

The product version 1 was released on December 2017 and version 2 in February 2019.
project Duration
Version 1 - 1 Month
Version 2 - 2 Month
Tools
Sketch, Zeplin, Visual Studio Code
